    FAQ

    Questions worth asking before you route.

    What is Lender Fit Routing Copilot?

    A Custom GPT that compares a redacted borrower profile with structured funding-product criteria and prepares transparent routing outputs for human review.

    Does the GPT guarantee funding approval?

    No. It is not a lender, approval engine, offer, guarantee, or underwriting decision.

    What information should I provide?

    A redacted summary covering the business profile, revenue, operating history, funding request, use of funds, credit range, obligations, and document readiness.

    How does product-fit scoring work?

    Products that survive applicable hard gates receive a transparent weighted score across eligibility, cash flow, purpose, risk, timing, documentation, and structure.

    Is the score an approval probability?

    No. A product-fit score measures internal alignment. It does not estimate or guarantee approval.

    What happens when no product fits?

    The GPT returns No Current Match, explains the blocking conditions, and identifies fixable gaps or structural conflicts.

    Can the GPT submit applications?

    No. It can prepare checklists and drafts, but submission and external writes require explicit human authorization.

    How current are the product criteria?

    The workflow displays freshness and verification status. Product availability and requirements may change and require current review.

    Is borrower information stored?

    The public workflow is designed for redacted structured information. Never provide restricted identifiers or credentials in ordinary chat.

    Who should use this GPT?

    Small-business owners, brokers, funding agencies, processors, referral partners, consultants, and fintech operators.
